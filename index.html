PhD Research Overview

From Policy Commitment to Institutional Enactment

Learner-centredness in Irish higher education through national document and policy analysis, comparative qualitative case study, and practice-based research.

Open presentation Read overview

Research focus

This PhD examines how learner-centredness is institutionally enacted in Irish higher education. The project is concerned with how learner-centred commitments are interpreted, negotiated and operationalised within institutional settings through governance processes, professional roles, strategic priorities and lived academic practice.

National policy analysis

Examines how learner-centredness is constructed within Irish higher education policy and sectoral discourse.

Comparative case study

Explores how learner-centred commitments are interpreted and enacted within two higher education institutions.

Analytic documentary artefact

Develops a practice-based artefact as part of the analytic process, not simply as a dissemination output.
